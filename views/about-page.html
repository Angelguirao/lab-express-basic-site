About Sam Harris

Sam Harris is a renowned author, neuroscientist, philosopher, and podcast host. He is widely recognized for his thought-provoking ideas and intellectual contributions.

Biography

Sam Harris was born on April 9, 1967, in Los Angeles, California. He obtained a degree in philosophy from Stanford University and a Ph.D. in neuroscience from the University of California, Los Angeles.

Books

  • "The End of Faith" (2004)
  • "Letter to a Christian Nation" (2006)
  • "The Moral Landscape" (2010)
  • "Waking Up: A Guide to Spirituality Without Religion" (2014)
  • "Free Will" (2012)
  • "Lying" (2013)

Podcast

Sam Harris hosts the popular podcast "Making Sense" where he engages in insightful conversations with leading intellectuals, scientists, and thinkers on a wide range of topics.
